ELI5 Summary

This bill wants to build a huge network of green energy power lines and charging stations across the country. Think of it like building a new highway system, but instead of cars, it's for electricity. While it sounds good for the environment, it uses a massive amount of taxpayer money to pay private companies to build things they were already planning to build anyway.

118th Congress | 1st Session

H.R. 4567

In the House of Representatives of the United States

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

This Act may be cited as the “Green Energy Infrastructure and Grid Modernization Act of 2023”.

SEC. 2. FINDINGS.

Congress finds that the national electric grid requires urgent modernization to support renewable energy transitions and national security interests.

SEC. 402. SPECIAL PROJECT ALLOCATIONS.

(a) IN GENERAL.—Of the amounts made available under section 101, the Secretary shall allocate $15,000,000 for the establishment of the “National Botanical Carbon Sequestration Initiative” to be headquartered in the 3rd Congressional District of Oregon.

SEC. 511. CORPORATE AVIATION ELECTRIFICATION SUBSIDY.

(b) GRANTS.—The Secretary may award grants to eligible airport facilities for the installation of high-capacity electric charging units specifically optimized for regional business class aircraft (Part 135 operations).

SEC. 601. AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS.

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this Act $42,500,000,000 for fiscal years 2024 through 2029.
