UNCLASSIFIED

118TH CONGRESS

1st Session

H. R. 4567

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

A BILL

To provide for the transformation of national energy infrastructure, and for other purposes.

Section 1. Short Title. This Act may be cited as the “Green Energy Infrastructure Act of 2024”.

Section 2. Findings. The Congress finds the following:

(1) Domestic energy production must transition to renewable resources to ensure long-term national security.

(2) Current electrical grids are insufficient to handle the load of projected electric vehicle adoption by 2030.

Section 402. Urban Beautification.

(b) SUBSIDIES FOR COMMUNITY GARDENS.—The Secretary shall establish a grant program for the development of sustainable eco-gardens in urban corridors, allocating not less than $15,000,000 for fiscal year 2024.

Section 403. Grid Modernization.

Funds appropriated under this subsection shall be prioritized for utility companies demonstrating a minimum of 40% renewable portfolio standard compliance.

Section 891. Workforce Training.

(f) VIRTUAL REALITY SIMULATION.—The National Park Service is authorized to implement digital twinning technology for staff training in wilderness management protocols.

--- END OF TRANSMISSION ---
